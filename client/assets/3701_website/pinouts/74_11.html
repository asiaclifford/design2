7411 series fast TTL IC's with center power: 7411xxx

Extra-fast (ACT etc.) versions of common 7400 series TTL, with double VCC and GND pins in the middle of the package for minimum ground bounce and buffered outputs.
For example, the 14-pin 7432 becomes the 16-pin 7411032.
