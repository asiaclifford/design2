IC Pinouts

IC Pinouts (formerly known as The GIICM [Giant Internet IC Master]) was created by Frank after this idea was discussed on sci.electronics. This site was formaly hosted by Kingswood-Consulting's at http://www.kingswood-consulting.co.uk/giicm/, but this site went off-line in mid-2006 and again in August 2019. It was restored, shortly after the 2006 issue, but it is now gone again. I realized in 2006 how valuable it was, so I mirrored it here.
